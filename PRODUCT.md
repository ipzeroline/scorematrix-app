# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Sports fans, football enthusiasts, and prediction game players who want live football data, AI insights, leaderboard competition, and reward redemptions.

## Product Purpose
A multilingual football prediction, live score, rewards, missions, and news dashboard that provides live match data, interactive prediction wagers, and a community space.

## Brand Personality
Professional, sleek, competitive, premium dark e-sports, high contrast, clean and performance-driven.

## Anti-references
Loud cyberpunk neon overload, excessive glowing borders, flashing scanlines, low-contrast text on dark surfaces, and over-stimulating animations.

## Design Principles
- **Content First**: Prioritize match data, scores, and statistics over decorative visual clutter.
- **Sleek Sports Aesthetics**: Use sharp borders, high-contrast states, and deep dark background surfaces with professional accent colors instead of neon glow effects.
- **Performance & Readability**: Keep text crisp, use accessible color contrasts, and ensure transitions are snappy and responsive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG Level AA compliance for text contrast.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion settings.
- Clean typography hierarchy using AuraSans as the primary typeface.
